Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Girl Math Wallet (“Girl Math Wallet,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is an early-stage investing education and portfolio insights app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit our website, join our waitlist, or use our app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Girl Math Wallet, we may collect:

  • Contact information, such as your email address if you join a waitlist or contact us.
  • Account information, such as profile settings, goals, preferences, and app activity.
  • Financial account data, if you choose to connect a brokerage through SnapTrade, including connected institution names, account display details, balances, holdings, positions, transactions, dividends, deposits, withdrawals, and related metadata.
  • Usage and device information, such as pages viewed, browser type, approximate location, IP address, device identifiers, diagnostics, and log data.
  • Content you provide, such as goal names, notes, support messages, and feedback.

2. Brokerage Connections Through SnapTrade

Girl Math Wallet may use SnapTrade to help you connect supported brokerages and integrations. Brokerage login and connection flows are handled through SnapTrade or the relevant financial institution. We do not ask for or store your brokerage username, password, or multi-factor authentication codes.

If you connect a brokerage, you authorize us and SnapTrade to access and process the brokerage data needed to provide portfolio insights, dividend summaries, spending-versus-investing analysis, goal tracking, and related app features. Your use of a SnapTrade-powered connection may also be subject to SnapTrade’s own terms and privacy notices.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, personalize, and improve Girl Math Wallet.
  • Display portfolio balances, holdings, dividends, account activity, goals, and projections.
  • Create playful insights, such as dividend treat equivalents and spending-versus-investing comparisons.
  • Send service messages, respond to support requests, and communicate with you about the app.
  • Analyze app performance, troubleshoot issues, prevent fraud, and protect the service.
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, security, and contractual obligations.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information with:

  • Service providers that help us host, secure, analyze, support, and operate the service.
  • SnapTrade and connected financial institutions, when needed to enable brokerage connections and account-data features.
  • Professional advisors, auditors, insurers, and legal or compliance providers.
  • Authorities or other parties when required by law, legal process, security needs, or to protect rights and safety.
  • Successors in connection with a merger, financing, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets.

We do not sell your personal financial account data.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our website and app may use cookies, pixels, SDKs, logs, and similar technologies to remember preferences, understand traffic, measure performance, and improve the product. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as expected if cookies are disabled.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No internet or storage system is guaranteed to be completely secure. Please contact us if you believe your interaction with Girl Math Wallet is no longer secure.

7. Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and protect the service. You may request deletion of your account or connected brokerage data by contacting us.

8. Your Choices

You can choose not to connect a brokerage. You may also disconnect a brokerage connection, unsubscribe from marketing emails, update certain account information, or request access, correction, deletion, or export of your information where applicable law provides those rights.

9. Children and Minors

Girl Math Wallet is not intended for children under 13. Brokerage connection features are intended only for users who are old enough to own or control the connected brokerage account and enter into these terms under applicable law.

10. International Transfers

Information may be processed in the United States, Canada, or other countries where we or our service providers operate. Data protection laws in those places may differ from the laws where you live.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above shows when this policy was most recently revised. Continued use of the service after changes means the updated policy applies.

12. Contact

Questions or requests can be sent to hello@girlmathwallet.app. Please do not include sensitive information, passwords, or full financial account numbers in email.
